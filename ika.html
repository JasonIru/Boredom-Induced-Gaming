Ikachan

Underwater adventure

Developer: Pixel

Genre: Action, Adventure

[Description WIP] Ikachan is a short 2d exploration game set underwater. There is no jumping and running as in other sidescrolling games, instead Ikachan must swim and propel himself forward towards his destination. The game features a rather simple but cute story, slow paced combat and light puzzle elements. It won't take long to finish this one, but it offers a unique experience unlike any other game out there. It's worth noting that this game was developed by Pixel of Cave Story fame and can be seen as a sort of prototype for it.
