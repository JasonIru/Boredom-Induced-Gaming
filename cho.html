Cho Ren Sha 68K

Arcade quality vertical shooter

Developer: Famibe no Yosshin

Genre: Arcade

[Description WIP] Cho Ren Sha 68K offers a polished arcade like experience coupled with a terrific soundtrack. The gameplay is very standard for this genre and has little in the way of gimmicks, however the stage, enemy and boss design make this title highly addictive. The only bit of critique that can be leveled against this game is that there is only one background for every stage. That hardly matters in the long run since the character graphics more than make up for that. The game repeats indefinetely which means skilled players can keep enjoying the game by aiming for a high score long after they completed a 1CC.
